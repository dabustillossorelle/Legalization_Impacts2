Further below is a snapshot of the current landscape on legalization, medical use, recreational use, and anything in between, with more on state laws available at the provided links.

Are you wondering what impacts legalization is having on people's health, well-being and financial standing?

We seek to touch on some of these topics with the data and charts provided. Tolerance seems to be growing steadily, with legalization continuing to spread. At the same time, communities seem highly reluctant to allow dispensaries to set up shop in their districts.
